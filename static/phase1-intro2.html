Section1 Instruction

After seeing their offers, you will be instructed to use your facial expression to express your emotion. Your facial expressions will be recorded and sent back to them as feedback.

The recording will start when you click "Record". The recoding will last for 3 seconds. If you are satisfied with the recording, you can click "Next" and move on to next step. If you are unsatisfied with this recording, you can do it again by clicking "Retry".

Next, when you submit the video successfully, you will be asked to report your emotional feelings on a scale, and how much money you want give back to that person out of your $X * 3 by typing in a number.
