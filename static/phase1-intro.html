Section1 Instruction

In this section, you will play the role of receiver. You will receive different offers ranging from $1 to $10 proposed by some previous participants. Their first name and photos will also be displayed. Each of them had $10 at the beginning and the money that person decided to share $X to you will be tripled ($X * 3) when you receive.

For example, if Alice decided to share $7 of her $10 to you, you will receive:

$7 * 3 = $21.
