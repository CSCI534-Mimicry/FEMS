Introduction

Welcome to FEMS! FEMS is a system designed to analyze people's emotion and their corresponding behaviors.

We are going to use FEMS to collect data via several rounds of a game. In this game, you will be paired with another participant, and each of you will play the role of giver or receiver. In each round, the giver need to give some of her money to the receiver. Then the receiver will decide to give some money back. More details will be explained later.
