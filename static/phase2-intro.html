Section2 Instruction

Congratulations! Section 1 is completed. Now, in Section 2, you will interact with virtual human agents.

At the beginning of each interaction, you will have $10 and you need to decide how much money you want to share with this agent. The money you decide to share ($X) will be tripled ($X * 3) when the agent receives. For example, if you decide to share $6 of your $10 to this agent, this agent will receive $6 * 3 = $18.

After receive the money you share, the agent will decide how much money they want to give back to you out of their $X * 3. Your payment will also be related to the results of their decisions. You will know their decisions at the end of the procedure.
